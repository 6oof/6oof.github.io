Colors:

This color scheme is ideal for a cybersecurity education startup because it combines the trustworthiness and reliability of deep blues with the fresh, forward-thinking energy of vibrant greens. The calming tones create a sense of security and professionalism, while the lively accents convey innovation and growth, reinforcing the startup's commitment to empowering users with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate the digital world safely.

Buttons:

Used a 6px border radius for buttons, inputs, and containers. This keeps a modern feel without being overly playful or too sharp. Rounded edges help soften the interface, making it more approachable, while still feeling structured.
